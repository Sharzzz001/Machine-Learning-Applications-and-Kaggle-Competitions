Automating Block Reason Classification Using AI Topic Modelling

In our IWM Operations environment, users often record block reasons as free-text notes when an account is placed on transfer block or total block. While this approach allows flexibility, it also creates challenges: the wording is inconsistent, phrasing varies across users, and reporting requires significant manual review. Similar issues arise with short abbreviations, typos, and different writing styles ‚Äî all of which make structured analysis difficult.

To address this, the team has implemented an AI-driven topic modelling solution that automatically converts these unstructured block notes into standardised, meaningful categories, significantly improving transparency and operational efficiency.

1. Discovering Natural Topics with BERTopic

The first phase involved using BERTopic, a modern NLP technique that groups semantically similar text entries without relying on exact keywords. Instead, it leverages context-aware embeddings, which capture the meaning of a sentence by analysing:
	‚Ä¢	Self-attention: how important each word is relative to others in the sentence
	‚Ä¢	Word embeddings: numerical representations of word meaning
